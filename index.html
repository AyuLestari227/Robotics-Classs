Let's Join With Us

Robotics is a branch of technology that deals with the design, construction, operation, structural disposition, manufacture, and application of robots. Robotics is related to the science of electronics, machines, mechanics, and computer software. A robot is an electronic device programmed to perform tasks automatically that would normally be performed by humans. In other words, robotics is a science that deals with the design and manufacture of robots. Not only that, robotics can also be defined as a branch of modern technology that designs, operates, constructs, manufactures, and applies robots as well. Although it is not easy to do, robotics is currently one of the fields of science that is increasingly popular, especially among young people. Why isn't it easy? Of course, when you want to make a robot, you need to study mechanics, electronics, algorithms, machines, and computer software. It's no wonder that now more and more schools and colleges make robotics a special and general subject.

Microcontroller

The microcontroller is a microcontroller chip. By adding input and output devices, the microcontroller can act as a microcomputer on a chip. The main advantage of a microcontroller is that it controls the operation of a machine or system using applications stored in ROM.
